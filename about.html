Who we are

Enactus is an international non-profit organization that nurtures managerial skills of students. It channelizes the energy of these future leaders towards constructing innovative business models that improve the quality of life and standard of living of the people in need. Enactus creates and implements community empowerment projects around the globe. The experience not only transforms lives, but also helps students develop the skills that are essential in this challenging, corporate world. Enactus is now collaborating with Jamia Millia Islamia University to expand this network across the globe.

Objective: Our main objective is to offer employment opportunities that empower and inspire persons to have a financial sustainability by means of enhancing their standard of living.
Societal Improvement: We work together in producing ground-breaking business ideas for the improvement and enrichment of the society, our main aim is societal enhancement.
Innovation: We believe in having an “outside the box” approach in order to provide novelty and freshness in sculpting new business projects.
Growth: We work for the personal growth of our members and believe in growth and empowerment of the weaker ones in the society.

Achievements

Centre For Innovation and Enterpreneurship

Innovate for a cause was organised by the Centre of Innovation and Entrepreneurship( CIE ) on 17th march 2016. Out of the 27 entries , 13 teams were shortlisted for the second round after which 6 teams were selected for the final round. After each presentation followed a question and answer round. Team Enactus presented their innovation - PROJECT VASUDHA and came through with flying colors and stood 1st in this years' competition. Our honourable Vice Chancellor Professor Talat Ahmad congratulated all the teams for their diligent efforts and finally presented team enactus the winning sum of Rs 12000 . It was a great competition which not only gave the students of jamia a great platform to present their ideas but also gave the students an amazing opportunity to learn.
